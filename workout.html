Routine 1: Full-Body workout

This workout targets all major muscle groups

  • Exercise: Squats | Sets: 3 | Reps: 12
  • Exercise: Push-ups | Sets: 3 | Reps: 10
  • Exercise: Lunges | Sets: 3 | Reps: 12 (each leg)

Routine 2: Upper Body Strength

This routine focuses on building upper body strength.

  • Exercise: Bench Press | Sets: 4 | Reps: 8
  • Exercise: Bent-over Rows | Sets: 4 | Reps: 10
  • Exercise: Shoulder Press | Sets: 3 | Reps: 12

Routine 3: Core Challenge

Strengthen your core with this challenging routine.

  • Exercise: Planks | Sets: 3 | Duration: 30 seconds
  • Exercise: Russian Twists | Sets: 3 | Reps: 15 (each side)
  • Exercise: Bicycle Crunches | Sets: 3 | Reps: 15

Routine 4: Lower Body Blast

This workout is designed to strengthen your legs and glutes.

  • Exercise: Deadlifts | Sets: 4 | Reps: 10
  • Exercise: Leg Press | Sets: 4 | Reps: 12
  • Exercise: Calf Raises | Sets: 3 | Reps: 15
