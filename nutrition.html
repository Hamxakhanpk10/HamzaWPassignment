Meal Planning

Effective meal planning can help you make healthier choices
TIPS

  • Plan your meals for the week ahead.
  • Incorporate a variety of foods.
  • Prepare meals in advance.
  • Use a shopping list to avoid impulse buys.

Healthy Eating Habits

healthy eating habits can improve your overall well-being

  • Eat more fruits and vegetables.
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • Limit sugar and saturated fats.
  • Stay hydrated with water.

Healthy Dishes

Try these healthy dishes to keep your meals exciting:

  • Quinoa Salad: Quinoa, cherry tomatoes, parsely, and green onion.
  • Grilled Chicken: Marinated chicken breast served with steamed broccoli.
  • Fruit Smoothie: Blend bananas, spinach, and almond milk.
